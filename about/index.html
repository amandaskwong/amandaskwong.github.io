    • About me!

    Amanda is an artist, designer, and bagel-lover from Calgary, Alberta.

    Amanda is a student at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT). There, she is pursuing her diploma in interactive design and majoring in graphic design. After graduating from SAIT, Amanda hopes to design merchandise and packaging. She strives to promote sustainability and accessibility while creating strong brand identities for her clients.

    When she isn't at school, Amanda enjoys planning dinners with her friends, drawing with her cat, and trying every bagel available in the city.
